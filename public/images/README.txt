THE JUNGLE WEY — Photo Assets
================================

Drop your restaurant photos here with these exact filenames:

  garden-1.jpg    — Main garden shot (wide table, teal chairs, trees, shade sail)
  garden-2.jpg    — Second garden angle (mural wall, lush trees)
  menu-food.jpg   — Food menu card photo (coral red design)
  menu-drinks.jpg — Drinks menu card photo
  detail-1.jpg    — Any atmosphere detail / close-up shot

Recommended: JPG format, minimum 1200px wide.

The website has beautiful SVG fallbacks if photos are not present,
but using your real photos will make it feel 10x more authentic.

After adding photos, just refresh the browser — no restart needed.
